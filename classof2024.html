SIMAD University’s 20th Graduation Ceremony

As the Media Specialist for SIMAD University’s 20th Graduation Ceremony, I designed and produced a wide range of media and branding materials, including the official graduation book, graduation report, stage and screen designs, social media campaigns, and printed collaterals. I also directed and edited a special short film documenting the impact of the Class of 2024, capturing their journey and achievements. My work ensured a unified visual identity and a memorable experience for graduates, guests, and the wider community.

Year

2024

Client

SIMAD University

Description

The 20th Convocation of SIMAD University celebrated two decades of excellence in higher education, graduating 781 students from diverse faculties.

The event brought together faculty, families, and distinguished guests to honor the Class of 2024, a generation of future leaders prepared to contribute to Somalia’s development.
